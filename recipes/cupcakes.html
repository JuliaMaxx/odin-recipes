Vanilla Cupcakes

Description:

This easy vanilla cupcake recipe is made with simple ingredients. They are very tasty!

Vanilla Cupcakes Ingredients:

  • ⅔ cup unsalted butter
  • ¾ cup White Sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 ½ cups All-purpose Flour

Steps:

  1. Preparation Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 12-cup muffin tin or line cups with paper liners.
  2. Make Batter Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in vanilla extract. Stir in flour just until mixed; spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full.
  3. Bake Bake in the preheated oven until the tops spring back when lightly pressed, 18 to 20 minutes. Cool in the tin briefly before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

How to Store Vanilla Cupcakes:

Always store your homemade cupcakes in an airtight container away from sunlight on your countertop for a day OR in the fridge for 4-6 days.
